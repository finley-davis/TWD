Texas Well Depth Project

Visualization of Aquifer Well Depths over the Past Century (1920-2023)

Project Overview

Texas aquifers are vital sources of groundwater for millions of people. This project visualizes well depth trends across major aquifers in Texas over the past century.

3-D Aquifer Animations

The goal of these plots is to illustrate the general trends in groundwater well depths.
